It was a dark and stormy night...

When out of nowhere, dinosaurs crashed through the trees!

Followed by a strange man in a spacesuit! "Shouldn't have done that," said the man.

I was confused by the semantic ambiguity of the man's statement. "You shouldn't have done that, or I shouldn't have done that?" I asked. Nevermind that I had no idea what I possibly could have done, other than camp in a spot that was improbably plagued by dinosaurs.
